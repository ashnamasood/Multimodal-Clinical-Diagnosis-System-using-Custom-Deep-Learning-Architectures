    How it works
    
            
    Model and training details
    
          
    
          
    
            
    01
    Chest X-ray (pneumonia)
    Custom CNN trained on the Kaggle chest X-ray pneumonia dataset for Normal vs Pneumonia classification with Grad-CAM explainability.
    
            
    02
    Skin and heart models
    HMNIST skin lesion CNN and UCI heart-disease MLP integrate when checkpoints are available on the server.
    
            
    03
    Fusion logic
    The backend merges modality outputs into a single risk score, recommendation, and supporting clinical notes.
    
          
    
          
    
            
    Inference flow: upload → preprocess → per-modality inference → confidence ranking → fused clinical summary.
    
            
    Limitations: models are research prototypes and must not replace expert medical judgment or formal reporting.
    
          
    
        
    

        
    
          
    
            
    FAQ
    
            
    Common questions
    
          
    
          
    
            What does the system do?
            
    It combines available modality outputs and presents a single review dashboard for clinical decision support.
    
          
    
          
    
            Can I use only the chest X-ray model?
            
    Yes. The interface handles missing skin or heart inputs and still returns chest X-ray results with fusion scoring where applicable.
    
          
    
          
    
            Why are some models unavailable?
            
    Skin and heart models are optional. If checkpoints are missing, the app continues with whichever models are loaded.
    
          
    
          
    
            Is this a medical device?
            
    No. This demo is for research and educational use and should not replace professional medical judgment.
